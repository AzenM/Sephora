- Audience: Fragrance / Store / Discounted

- Value pool: EUR 329,759

## Why this matters

This cohort is worth about EUR -24 more than the median recruited cohort.

## Recommended move

Keep the cohort but swap blanket discounts for bundles, samples, or exclusivity.

## Message and channel design

- Channel: Acquisition + CRM onboarding
- Offer strategy: Replicate the first-basket mix and the first 30-day welcome sequence
- Message angle: Acquire more customers who start the right routine, not just more customers

## Deep dive questions

- Which acquisition cohorts create the strongest twelve month value and margin profile?
